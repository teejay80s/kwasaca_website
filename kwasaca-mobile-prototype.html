About
The Apex Body for
Kwara's HIV Response
KWASACA is the state-level coordinating agency for all HIV/AIDS activities in Kwara State, delivering a multisectoral response spanning prevention, treatment, research, and community engagement.

Government-led Coordination

Chaired by the Kwara State Deputy Governor, ensuring high-level political commitment and oversight.

International Partnerships

PEPFAR, Global Fund, World Bank, UNAIDS, WHO — collaborating to strengthen Kwara's health systems.

Statewide LACA Network

16 Local Action Committees across all LGAs ensuring grassroots reach and community ownership.
